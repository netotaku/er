Eastville Running Club

Bristol, BS5. #runBS5

We're a growing group of people, based in Eastville Bristol UK, who enjoy running and taking on a challenge, we are definitely a mixed ability bunch, with varying distances and pace capabilities but ultimately enjoy the support and camaraderie a club brings.

Fund raising for Glenfrome Primary

We are on a mission to raise £1500 for Glenfrome Primary School. We're a group of parents who have dabbled with a bit of running, to varying degrees of success. But now we’re ready to take on a mega challenge: The Bristol Half Marathon, and in doing so raise some serious cash for our fantastic local primary school.
